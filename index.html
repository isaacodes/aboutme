Design Principles
When I design a website, I tend to stick to a very minimalistic theme. If too much is going on on a webpage, a user can feel lost, and I know that that feeling is not a pleasant one. To lessen this effect on my users, I like to make my websites easy to navigate while still maintaining a pleasing look and feel.
